About Me

I am a qualified and experienced interpreter and translator specialising in Georgian, Russian, and English. With over 15 years on the National Register of Public Service Interpreters (NRPSI), I bring extensive expertise across legal, medical, court, immigration, and tribunal settings. My NRPSI Registration Number is 14799. I work across the UK in person and remotely. My work is guided by accuracy, cultural sensitivity, and confidentiality.
