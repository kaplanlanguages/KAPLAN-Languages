About Me

I am a qualified and experienced interpreter and translator specialising in Georgian, Russian, and English. With over 15 years on the National Register of Public Service Interpreters (NRPSI), I bring extensive expertise across legal, medical, court, immigration, and tribunal settings. My NRPSI Registration Number is 14799. I regularly work in judicial, immigration, and tribunal proceedings throughout the UK. I offer accurate, reliable language services tailored to client needs, with a firm commitment to cultural sensitivity, confidentiality, and professional excellence.
